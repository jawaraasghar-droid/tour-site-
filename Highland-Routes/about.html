About Highland Routes

A promise that started at home

Based in Skardu, Gilgit-Baltistan — built on a habit older than the business itself.

Our story began with a simple family habit: whenever visitors arrived in Skardu, we would help them find the safest road, the warmest meal, and the quietest viewpoint. Over time, those small acts became a promise.

Today Highland Routes welcomes travelers with the same spirit. We plan practical routes, arrange comfortable stays, listen to what each guest needs, and create days that feel personal instead of rushed.
